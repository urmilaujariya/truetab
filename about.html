Get To Know Us

We are dreamers, innovators and tech enthusiasts who are
on amission to solve real lifechallenges faced by local
pharmacies and customers.

India’s first and only
tech & service platform
for local pharmacies.

After conducting research amongst pharmacies across India we were able to conclude that every local pharmacy is struggling to fight the discounts & offers battle with networked and funded pharmacies. While local pharmacies want to launch their own online stores, improve/introduce home delivery services, execute online marketing and build customer loyalty – most of them don’t have the time or expertise to do so.

A Single Platform for all your pharmacy needs.

Our goal is to help every local pharmacy with technology and service support. We take traditional pharmacies from offline to online & empower them with the tech and services to stay relevant in today’s everchanging environment. These tech and service initiatives are designed to help local pharmacies grow their business and win customers for life.

Our Vision

Enthuse respect and care to healthcare providers and end consumers

Our Mission

By empowering healthcare service providers and consumers with technology – one pharmacy and one consumer at a time.
